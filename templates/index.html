Emotion-Based Music Player

Welcome to the Emotion-Based Music Player. This application uses your webcam to detect your emotions and plays music that matches your mood.

Click the button below to start the emotion detection process. Ensure your webcam is enabled and positioned correctly for the best experience.

How It Works

  1. Click the "Start Emotion Detection" button.
  2. Allow access to your webcam when prompted.
  3. The system will analyze your facial expressions and detect your emotion.
  4. Press "q" on your keyboard to capture your emotion.
  5. Music matching your current mood will be played.
